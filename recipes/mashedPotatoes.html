Lasagna

Description

These garlic mashed potatoes are rich and very tasty! .

For a creamier texture, use heavy cream in place of the milk. These are also terrific topped with sesame seeds or green onions.

Ingrediants

  • Potatoes
  • Milk and Butter
  • Garlic
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Adding the butter first to the hot potatoes allows you to use less milk/cream and keeps the potatoes creamier for longer.
  2. Cooking the potatoes with garlic allows the garlic flavor to permeate the potatoes and sweetens the garlicâ€™s flavor rather than using raw, harsh garlic at the end. You can use russet potatoes, but they will not be as creamy as they are starchier.
  3. Serve with any protein that has a gravy.
  4. Stir in some sour cream and chives to add extra creaminess and flavor!
